The Layout

The SoA that you have just exported is based on the Rooms you selected in the MS Access form SoA_Input_Form. You will be presented with a Summary page and tabs named for the Areas that the rooms you chose have come from.

The formulas and formatting should be applied dynamically so there shouldnt be too much to do other than any specific things you want to add in or change.

The Summary tab

The Summary tab is where you can see a summary of the Departments and Sub Departments that you selected.

  • The Target NET (m2) column is a sub total of each Sub Department from across the other tabs.
  • Planning allowances (%) is set to 5% on all rows. This can be changed if you have a specific allowance for a particular Sub Department and the rest of the formulas will adjust themselves accordingly.
  • Planning allowance Total (m2) is calculated based on the information in the Target NET (m2) column.
  • Engineering allowance (%) is set to 5% on all rows. This can be changed if you have a specific allowance for a particular Sub Department and the rest of the formulas will adjust themselves accordingly.
  • Engineering allowance Total (m2) is calculated based on the information in the Target NET (m2) column.

The Other Tabs

The other tabs will be formatted identically to each other and will contain the following headers:
AL3, AL2, AL1, Room_ID, Room_Description, Qty, Area, Total_Area, Comments1.

  • AL3, AL2, AL1 represent Area, Department and Sub Department from the SoA_Input_Form. The AL3 and AL1 columns are used in creating the Summary tab.
  • Room_ID is the unique identifier for each room as set in the database2.
  • Room_Description is the name of the room.
  • The Qty data has been taken from the Template SoA3. It can be changed and all formulas will adjust themselves accordingly.
  • Area is the total area of the room as set in the database. The information has come from the Template SoA3.
  • Total Area is the total area of the room multiplied by the Qty.
  • Comments have come from the Template SoA and are in the database3.

    • 1As this is still an early version these column headers are likely to change in the future.

    2This column is just for debug purposes at this stage and may be removed

    3This data can easily be changed in the database should any of it be consistently wrong or just in need of updating. See the reference Template SoA for further details.

Notable information

Columns K:L are reserved for forumlas needed to fill the Summary Tab. The Summary uses a Vlook-up that checks those columns so any changes there may break the Summary page

Formatting is created for each tab as the worksheet is created. The formulas are created dynamically based on the information passed on from the SoA_Input_Form. The data should be accurate but its always wise to check the numbers as the script isnt clever enough to determine errors, let alone fix them as it goes.
