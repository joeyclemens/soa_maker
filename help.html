Getting Started

When you open the SoA_Template Access file, you'll be prompted to choose between creating a new SoA or adding to an existing one.
Starting a new SoA will permanently delete the previous SoA that was being worked on in this database.

Lets build a SoA

Regardless of whether you're starting a new SoA or continuing an existing one, you'll be directed to the input form called SoA_Input_Form. (If you accidentally close it, you can find it under that name in the MS Access Navigation bar).
You'll be prompted to select the Area(s), Department(s) and Sub-Department(s) and Room(s) you want to add to the SoA:

  • Begin by selecting the Area. It's recommended to tackle one at a time, but you can choose to work on as many as you like.
  • Then select the Department(s), Sub-Department(s) and Room(s). All this information is generated dynamically based on your selections.
  • When you have selected your rooms, press the "Add to SoA" button.
  • You can go back and add rooms from other areas, departments or sub-departments at any time. When you press the "Add to SoA" button any new entires will be added to the table "SoA".
  • You can view the rooms that have been added by pressing the "View SoA" button.
  • If you think you've made a series of terrible mistakes and just want to start again, press the "Start a fresh SoA" button. This will delete all content in the table so you can start again.
  • Or if you made a tiny mistake and just want to amend a room, press the "View SoA" button and just delete the row that contains the errant room.

Time to export

Are you ready to export your beautifully curated set of rooms into a fully functional SoA in MS Excel?

To do so, press the "Build the Soa" button. It may appear as if nothing is happening but in the background magic is happening.

You will be greeted with a pop up box asking to confirm if you want to continue. If you do then you will be given a file prompt. Choose where you want to save the SoA and what you want to call it and it will open automatically when its ready.

The SoA

You should be presented with the SoA you just created and the rooms that you selected previously. For a run down on how it works, press the "SoA template Export" button at the top of this page.
